Brief Description:

We are Urban Fresh, a UAE-based online grocery store that is specialized in selling healthy, fresh products. Our website includes several different categories ranging from fruits, vegetables, meats, poultry, and more.

As an eco-friendly vendor, we exclusively deliver our goods in paper bags to show how much we care about the environment. We strive in having our website user-friendly for everyone.
